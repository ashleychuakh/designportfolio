Travel Journey

Overview-

A personal website on my travel journey was designed and developed as part of a web development module (Web Developement and Design) undertaken in Australian National University during my exchange stint. The focus of the website will be on travelling, where I documented down the countries and cities that I have visited, including the next travel destinations.

Conceptual process-

In the initial stages of the project, I made a sketch of the envisioned travel website (with lo-fidelity) with Balsamiq. I chose to use Balsamiq instead of Adobe XD as I wanted a sketch of the idea, and there is no need for interactions between the different pages (the end product would be a rather static website).

In the later part of the assignment, it was required for us to develop a video on the topic of our website. In my case, it was travelling. I tried to make a compilation of the short videos that I took in the past (which were rather little), incorporating some still photos.

Storyboarding of the video was also conceptualized for this phase of the project and designed using canva.com

Reflections-

As I was designing and conceptualizing the product, I wondered about the colour theme that I will be using for this website. However, I have decided to go with the minimalistic colours of black and white as I envisioned that there will be multiple contrasting and conflicting colours from the images uploaded. Having a simple colour scheme will allow the images to stand out more.
